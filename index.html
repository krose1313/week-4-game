Crystal Collector Game!

A random number is displayed at the start of the game.


There are four magical crystals for you to click on. Each crystal represents a hidden number.

Each time you click on one of the four crystals you will add the number of points that crystal is worth to your total point counter.


Your goal is to match your point counter to the random number and you win!


If the points you generate exceed the random number then you lose


After a win or loss the game will reset and the values of each magical crystal will change. The random number will also change.
